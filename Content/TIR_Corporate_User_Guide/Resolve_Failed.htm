Resolve Failed

A failed record means that a record is unable to be imported into the system. A failed record differs from a record with errors in that a record with errors is successfully imported while a failed record is not.

To resolve a failed record, perform the following steps:

1.   Begin by resolving the issue within the Excel file and reimport.
2.   From the Import Results Summary, click the Failed card.

3.   The Failed sliding panel will appear, containing the records and reason for failure upon import.

Note: The record and reason for failure cannot be clicked into and edited here as the record has Failed, therefore it is not yet imported into the system. Resolve the issue(s) within the Excel file and reimport.
