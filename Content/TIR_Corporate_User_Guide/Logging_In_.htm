Logging In

To log into the Sovos TIR-Corporate application, perform the following steps:

1.   Navigate to the following URL: https://tir.sovos.com.
2.   On the login page, enter your Username and Password.

Note: Three invalid login attempts will result in that account being disabled for 30 minutes.

3.   If you’ve forgotten your password, click Forgot Your Password?. Enter the email address associated with the your account and click Send Email.

a. You’ll receive an email from no-reply-platform=sovos.com@platform.certmanageremail.com to reset your password. Click Reset Password to continue.

b.   You will be prompted to enter a previously chosen security question. Enter the appropriate Answer and click Submit.

Note: If you’ve forgotten this information, please contact your System Administrator or Sovos Support.

c.   Fill in the Password and Confirm password fields, click Submit, and log back into the application.
